I work at Purdue University, where I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Economics.

I am an applied microeconomist who uses game theory and experiments to understand how people and firms behave. My research intersects with labor economics and industrial organization. For more information, click on the tabs to see my research papers, CV and research statement.
